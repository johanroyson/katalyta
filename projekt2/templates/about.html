What it is

Presto Daycare is a digital toolbox that makes child care simpler, more efficient and more communicative. It has been developed together with teachers, pre-school staff, managers and parents, and is available through commonplace platforms web, tablets and smartphones. Streamline and simplify the daily administrative and pedagogical workflow at the pre-school while ensuring quality in communication with parents and guardians. Presto Daycare provides you with the right conditions to work proactively (in a demanding environment).

Through "apps" Presto Daycare handles daily activities, internal information and communication with parents. The staff manage daily administrative and pedagogical routines through Tablets and Smartphones. Documentation of relevance becomes available to parents (after staff OK) through free-to-download Presto Home (parent app) on their smartphones and thereby allowing access to information and features such as calendar, schedule and events, pictures, absence and illness reporting as well as specific information about their child.

What it does

Presto Daycare gives you seamless and simultaneous documentation of administrative routines and pedagogical tasks together with quality assured and accurate internal and external communication.

How it's done

Minimum requirements for operating Presto Daycare are as follows:


Preso Web is accessible to staff/management via Internet browser and handles base data (children, staff, parents), metrics, KPIs, statistics, reports and time schedules for staff (and parents)


Presto School is accessible to staff/management via iPad and Smartphone and handles main administrative pre-school processes, calendars, notifications etc.


Presto Home is accesible to parents via Smartphone and handles reporting, instructions and information from staff to parents
